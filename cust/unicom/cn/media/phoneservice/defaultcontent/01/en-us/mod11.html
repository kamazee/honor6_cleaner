• EXPAND SUSPEND BUTTON

    Click to expand suspend button
    Enable the suspend button from Settings. The button will then appear on most screens.The suspend button can be dragged to the left or right for easier access.Click to expand suspend button, it will show common functions including Back, Home, Suspend window,Lock screen and One-key optimization.

  • ROTATE SUSPEND MENU

    Slide to rotate menu
    Touch the suspend window button to enter the second-level menu, where you can slide the panel to view all suspend windows, including Music Player, Video Player, Messages, Notes, Calculator,and Calendar.
