Making a call
  1. Open Dialer.
  2. On the dialer, you can:
    • Enter the initials or first few letters of a contact's name, or part of the contact's phone number. Then select the contact you want to call from the search results.
    • Touch Contacts, select the contact you want to call from the contacts list, and touch .
    • From the call log, touch the contact you want to call.
