Backing up data
  1. Open Backup.
  2. Choose where you want to back up the data. By default, data is backed up to the internal storage. Touch to back up data to Cloud Drive or a USB storage device.
  3. Choose where you want to back up the data. By default, data is backed up to the internal storage. Touch to back up data to Cloud Drive.
  4. Choose whether to encrypt the backup file, and touch New backup.
  5. Select the data you want to back up, and follow the onscreen instructions to back it up.
