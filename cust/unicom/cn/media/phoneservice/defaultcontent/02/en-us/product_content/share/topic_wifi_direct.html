WLAN Direct

Connecting two devices using WLAN Direct

  1. Open the notification panel.
  2. Touch and hold from the shortcut switches to enter the WLAN settings screen.
  3. Turn on WLAN.
  4. Touch WLAN direct to scan for available devices.
  5. Select a device.

Sending a file using WLAN Direct

  1. Open File Manager.
  2. Touch and hold the file you want to send, and choose WLAN direct from the Share option menu.
