Calendar

With Calendar, you can:

  • Create an event: Touch , enter event details, such as the event name, location, time, and how often it repeats, and save the event.
  • View events: Touch the tabs on the top of the screen to switch between the year, month, week, day, and agenda views. The agenda view displays all your calendar events.
  • Share an event: Select the event you want to share, and touch to share the event using Bluetooth, email, or other methods.
