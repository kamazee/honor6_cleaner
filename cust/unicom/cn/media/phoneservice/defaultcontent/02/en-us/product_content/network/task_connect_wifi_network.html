Connecting to a WLAN
  1. Open the notification panel.
  2. Touch and hold from the shortcut switches to enter the WLAN settings screen.
  3. Turn on WLAN. Your phone then scans for available WLANs and displays them.
  4. Touch a WLAN to connect to it.
    • If the network is open, you will directly connect to it.
    • If the network is encrypted, enter the password when prompted.
