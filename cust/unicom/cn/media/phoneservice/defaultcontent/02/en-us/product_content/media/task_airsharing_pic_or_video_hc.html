Sharing a photo or video
  1. Open Gallery.
  2. Touch the photo or video you want to share.
  3. Flick up on the photo or video to turn on Transfer.
  4. Touch START. Your phone then automatically enables Bluetooth and searches for nearby Bluetooth devices.
  5. From the search results, touch the receiving device's name.

After the two devices are paired for the first time, flick up on a photo or video to quickly share it.
