Alarms

Open Clock. Under Alarm, you can:

  • Add an alarm: Touch , and set the alarm time, ringtone, how often it repeats, and more. Then touch DONE.
  • Turn on or turn off an alarm: Touch the switch next to an alarm to turn it on or off.
  • Configure alarm settings: Touch , and set the snooze duration, whether the alarm rings in silent mode, and what the volume buttons do when pressed.
  • Delete an alarm: Touch , select the alarm you want to delete, and touch DELETE.
