Why can't my phone open some audio clips or videos?
  • Your phone may not support the file format. Supported video and audio formats include MP3, WAV, OGG, MID, AMR, MP4, and 3GP.
  • The file may be corrupted. Download another copy of the file and try opening it again.
  • The file may be copyright-protected. Obtain a non copyright-protected version of the file.
