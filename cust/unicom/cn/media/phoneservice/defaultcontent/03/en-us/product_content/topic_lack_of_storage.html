What can I do if my phone is running out of storage space?
  • Open Phone Manager, touch Storage cleaner, and follow the onscreen instructions to clear the cache, remove unwanted applications and their installation packages, and delete large files.
  • If you have inserted a microSD card into your phone, move your photos, songs, videos, and other files to the microSD card.
  • Back up important data and restore your phone to its factory settings.
