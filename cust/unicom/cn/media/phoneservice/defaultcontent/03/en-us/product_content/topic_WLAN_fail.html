What can I do if I can't connect to a WLAN hotspot?
  • Check whether the WLAN hotspot name or password has changed. If so, obtain the new information and try again.
  • Make sure you are within the working range of the WLAN hotspot; otherwise the WLAN connection will automatically disconnect.
  • Some public WLAN hotspots may need to be connected through a web page. For details, contact the WLAN hotspot provider.
