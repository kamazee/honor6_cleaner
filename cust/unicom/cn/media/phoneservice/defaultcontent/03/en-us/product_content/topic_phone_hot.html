What can I do if my phone is overheating?

It is normal for smartphones to generate heat while you are charging, playing games, surfing the Internet, making calls, or using other power-intensive functions.

Your phone is designed with overheating protection for its circuits, helping prevent any heat damage to your battery.

  • Do not surf the Internet, play games, watch videos, or use other power-intensive application while your phone is charging.
  • Do not make calls or surf the Internet for long periods of time in places with poor reception.
  • Do not run too many applications simultaneously.
